Braised Chuck Roast

Simple Delicious Braised Chuck Roast

This recipe is incredibly easy to make for a dinner, lunch or meal prep. Follow the instructions below to make your own!
 
    Ingredients

  • Salt + Pepper
  • Chuck Roast (2-4lb)
  • Beef Stock or Bone Broth (Beef works best but you can subsitute chicken)
  • (Optional) Carrots, Potatoes, Rosemary
 
    Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 300 degrees fahrenheit
  2. Add a generous layer of salt and pepper to the chuck roast. Fully coat the meat, and let it sit for 30 mins.
  3. Place your dutch oven on the stove, set to medium-high heat. Add some oil or tallow.
  4. When the pan/oven is very hot, sear the roast 2-3 mins per side.
  5. Once the roast is seared, pour about 4 Cups of your stock/broth into the dutch oven. You want the broth to be about halfway covering the roast. At this point you can also add your potatoes, carrots, rosemary to stew with the roast!
  6. Cover your roast and place it in the oven for 4-5 hours.
  7. After 4-5 hours, remove the roast, shred apart, and enjoy!
