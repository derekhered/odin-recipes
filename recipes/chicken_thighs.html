Crispy Air Fryer Chicken Thighs

Simple, yet delicious chicken thigh method

This is one of the best ways to easily cook crispy, juicy chicken thighs. No need to add unneccessary oils, all you need is an air fryer and some seasonings!

 
    Ingredients

  • Salt, Paprika, Pepper, Garlic Powder, Parsley
  • 1-1.5lb Boneless Chicken Thighs
  • 1 TBPS. Sweet Chili Sauce
  • 2 TBPS. Soy Sauce
  • 15g Siracha
  • 50g Honey
    •
 
    Steps

  1. (Optional Dry Brine) Salt your chicken thighs generously, place them on a wire rack and store in the fridge for 6-8 hours, or overnight.
  2. Prepare your marinade, whisk together chili sauce, siracha, honey. Sprinkle in some garlic powder, paprika, pepper, and parsley.
  3. Pour marinade over chicken and fully coat in marinade. Let it sit for ATLEAST 1 hour. 2-4 hours is optimal.
  4. Place chicken thighs in air fryer.
  5. Cook at 380 degrees fahrenheit for 12 mins. Flip sides and repeat.
  6. Slice and serve!
