Mexican-Style Beans

Fajita style black beans

A recipe that can be made as a topping or a side! Great for a vegetarian option as well.

 
    Ingredients

  • Garlic powder + Salt
  • 4-6 Strips of bacon (substitute with oil for a vegetarian option)
  • 2 Cups Chicken stock
  • 1 Sweet Onion
  • Green, Yellow, or Red Bell Peppers (2-3 total)
  • 1 Can of black beans
 
    Steps

  1. Slice up your bacon strips into small pieces. If using oil instead of bacon, just place 2tbps. in a pan on medium heat and skip to step 3.
  2. Heat your pan on medium-high, cook the bacon pieces, stirring until crisp and brown.
  3. While the bacon is cooking, slice up your onion and peppers.
  4. Remove the individual bacon pieces from the pan, leaving the fat in it still.
  5. Sear your peppers and onions in the pan with the bacon fat until golden brown.
  6. Once the veggies are cooked, mix the can of beans with it, with the liquid. Stir for 3-4 mins.
  7. Place your chicken stock in with the veggies
  8. Turn heat down to low, let simmer for 15-30 mins or until the stock has thickened.
  9. Serve however you'd like, and enjoy!
